About Me

Hi. I'm a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University , where I am being advised by Muhammad Shahbaz. My research focuses on designing the next generation hybrid (software-hardware) abstractions and architectures for emerging Network Security and Telemetry Applications.

Before coming to Purdue University, I worked on projects ranging from Network and Systems Security to Human Computer Interaction with several distinguished faculty members at Lahore University of Management Sciences.
